# LBS CV Rules Integrated In The App

Source files provided by the user:

- `Action words for CVs.pdf`
- `CAR Model Template and example.pdf`
- `Personal qualities and core competencies.pdf`
- `LBS CV Guidelines.pdf`
- `Example CV with comments.pdf`

Implemented product rules:

- Use the LBS Word template for generated DOCX files.
- Target one page by prioritising relevant content instead of dumping every detail.
- Keep Education, Business Experience, Projects and Leadership, and Additional Information in LBS order.
- Keep Education and Business Experience in reverse chronological order.
- Put academic awards and distinctions under Education.
- Use 12-15 total Business Experience bullets where possible, typically 3-4 per role.
- Rewrite selected bullets toward CAR shape: Challenge/context, Action, Result.
- Start bullets with strong action verbs and avoid weak starts such as "responsible for" or "worked on".
- Preserve only real metrics; never invent results.
- Prefer transferable skills and business impact over technical jargon.
- Prioritise role-relevant bullets and keywords from the job description.
- Put languages in Additional Information and normalise proficiency to Native, Fluent, or Basic.
- Flag repeated action verbs, missing quantification, weak CAR structure, and missing role keywords during analysis.
