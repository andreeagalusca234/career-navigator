# 3-Minute Video Script

## Title

Career Navigator: Gemma 4 CV Coach for Digital Equity

## 0:00-0:20 - The Problem

Show a student looking at a rough CV and a competitive job description.

Narration:
"For many students, the problem is not talent. It is access. Premium career coaching is expensive, and generic CV tools do not understand local context, language barriers, or what recruiters actually look for."

## 0:20-0:40 - The Product

Show the landing screen.

Narration:
"Career Navigator is a bilingual CV coach powered by Gemma 4. It helps Romanian-speaking students turn an existing CV and a target job description into a recruiter-ready CV."

## 0:40-1:10 - Upload Flow

Show uploading a PDF or DOCX CV. Show the chat summarizing what it found.

Narration:
"The user uploads a CV. The app extracts education, experience, projects, skills, and languages. It does not ask for information that is already in the CV."

## 1:10-1:35 - Job Description + Gemma 4

Paste a target job description. Show the Gemma 4 badge and the assistant confirming the extracted role.

Narration:
"Gemma 4 reads the job description and extracts the role, skills, responsibilities, recruiter signals, and missing evidence areas. The fast path uses `gemma-4-26b-a4b-it`."

## 1:35-2:05 - Analysis

Click "Analyze my CV." Show score, top fixes, missing evidence, role fit, and bullet rewrite examples.

Narration:
"For analysis, Gemma 4 evaluates the CV against the role and LBS-style CV rules. It identifies weak bullets, missing evidence, and recruiter risks. Most importantly, it is instructed not to invent achievements or metrics."

## 2:05-2:35 - Generate CV

Click "Generate tailored CV." Show preview/download card and open the generated `.docx`.

Narration:
"The final tailoring step uses Gemma 4 reasoning, while a deterministic renderer creates the Word document. This keeps the language adaptive, but the formatting reliable."

## 2:35-2:50 - Accessibility

Show RO/EN switch, microphone, speaker button, and Romanian voice setting if available.

Narration:
"The platform switches between Romanian and English, supports dictation, and can read answers aloud. This reduces friction for users who are more comfortable speaking than typing."

## 2:50-3:00 - Closing

Show the final CV and the app side by side.

Narration:
"Career Navigator turns hidden career knowledge into an accessible workflow: upload, understand, improve, and apply. Powered by Gemma 4, built for digital equity."

## Recording Checklist

- Keep the final video under 3 minutes.
- Use a public YouTube link that does not require login.
- Avoid showing private email addresses, phone numbers, or API keys.
- Show the live demo URL, public repo URL, and generated DOCX.
